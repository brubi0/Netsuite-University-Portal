Week 1: Foundation

Building Blocks of SuiteFlow Automation

Welcome to Week 1!

This week lays the groundwork for your SuiteFlow journey. You'll start by understanding the fundamental concepts that underpin all workflow automation in NetSuite, then dive into the Workflow Manager to create your first workflow.

By the end of Week 1, you'll understand how workflows are structured, how triggers control action execution, and how the visual workflow development tool transforms business processes into automated solutions.

üéØ Week 1 Learning Objectives

  • Describe the foundational components of a business process and their representation in SuiteFlow
  • Navigate the Workflow Manager and understand its key components
  • Define workflow triggers and explain their role in controlling action execution
  • List the building blocks of workflows (states, actions, transitions, triggers)
  • Create a simple workflow and test it in your NetSuite training account
  • Explain the benefits of modular workflow design

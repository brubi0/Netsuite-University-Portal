Week Overview

NetSuite provides a comprehensive suite of standard reports to help you track and monitor your business in real time. This week covers everything you need to know about accessing, viewing, filtering, and distributing these built-in reports. You'll learn to navigate report data, apply dynamic filters, export in multiple formats, schedule recurring distributions, and use the "Alert Me When Ready" feature for large reports.

Learning Objectives

  • Access standard reports using global search, menus, and the Reports Dashboard
  • Navigate between summary and detail views with drill-down capabilities
  • Apply date ranges, subsidiary context, and column filters
  • Export reports in Excel, PDF, CSV, and Word formats
  • Schedule reports to run and distribute automatically
  • Use Alert Me When Ready for long-running reports

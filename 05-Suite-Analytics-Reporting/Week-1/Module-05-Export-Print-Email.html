Emailing Reports

Email reports directly from NetSuite to share data with colleagues or external recipients.

üìß Email Report Steps

  1. Click the Email icon in the report toolbar
  2. You are automatically added as a recipient
  3. Add additional recipients under Copy Others or type email addresses
  4. On the Message subtab, add your email message
  5. Choose the attachment format:
    • Inline HTML ‚Äî Report displays in the email body
    • Attachment ‚Äî Report attached as Excel, PDF, CSV, or Word
  6. On the Attachment subtab, optionally attach additional documents
  7. Click Send

üí° Pro Tip

You can email reports to both internal NetSuite users and external recipients (like clients or vendors) by entering their email addresses directly. You can also attach additional files from your computer or the NetSuite File Cabinet.

üéØ Key Takeaways

  • Attach additional documents when emailing reports
